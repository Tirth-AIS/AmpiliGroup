FAQ's

   1. What does Ampilli Construction cover ?

We cover all kinds of Construction, Renovation, and Interior projects

   2. Why should anybody choose Ampilli Constructions ?

The Ampilli brand and our team of qualified professionals stand for the Best standards of work quality. We will ensure timely delivery and will manage everything from plan approval to handover. There is an extensive warranty post-completion as well.

   3. Does Ampilli handle government plan approvals ?

Ampilli will assist you in obtaining all the relevant government approvals at a cost.

   4. How do you monitor quality ?

Quality will be monitored by the Quality Controller. Also, CCTV and 360-degree cameras will be installed at the site to access online viewing from any part of the world. Images will be shared on the Whatsapp group at the end of the day to share updates.

   5. How do you guarantee hassle-free work ?

The work is hassle-free because we are candid and maintain transparency at each and every level, starting from the procuring of materials, payments till execution. Also, the same will be updated to you by our relationship manager on a regular basis at your convenience. The Quality control team ensures that the quality is not compromised.

   6. How comfortable is your payment process ?

Our payments are very unique. We don’t take payments in stages; we take payments on actual work so that the client does not have to worry whether they are paying more than the actual work done. We also give cash flow details as per the work.

   7. Who will select materials and how will it get delivered at the site ?

The structure materials we use are from reputed brands and will be used once approved by You. All the finished materials will be finalized in the presence of the You and our Architect. Once finalized, the delivery of the materials will be coordinated and updated to the You through images.

   8. How will the workforce be assigned to a site ?

There will be one Site Supervisor available at the site throughout the assignment. Quality Engineers will visit the site at regular intervals and Senior Engineers will be visiting the site twice a week. Designers and Architects will be visiting the site at the beginning of any new stage as well as per the site requirements

   9. How is the project delay handled ?

The occurrence of project delays is rare however, we mention the penalty clause in the agreement and we stand by it strictly.

   10. How are the laborers and their stay managed?

Laborers and their stay are organized by Ampilli. As per the stay, labor sheds are set up on the site or at the nearest available site.

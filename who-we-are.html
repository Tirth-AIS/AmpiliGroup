About us

Ampili Constructions is one of the fastest growing and most admired House Construction companies in Hyderabad, India. The company was founded by A. Anjaiah on the 18th of October 2020 who has more than 30 years of experience in residential and commercial constructions. He has been successful in completing more than 250 projects across a range of sectors.

To pursue A. Anjaiah’s dreams further, his son A. Ashok stepped into the business to build a legacy for his employees and clients. With a modest beginning, A. Ashok started his career as a civil contractor and has 8 years of profound experience and excellent exposure across the construction industry.

Ampili Constructions is driven to deliver projects on-time with uncompromising standards of Quality, Safety and Sustainability. We have the expertise in constructing Individual houses, Residential Apartments, Commercial Spaces and Buildings, Villas and Duplex-Houses. We also provide turnkey solutions in Renovation and Interior Designing. We are strongly committed to labor and employee safety at project sites and workplace. Working with integrity, transformed us to spread our wings beyond horizon whilst maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety. Our passion and the foundation of a hard-earned reputation is what is moving us ahead.
